Our 42 rooms luxury Hotel situated In Accra Ghana. With a unique atmosphere, Hotel Green raises the bar for business and leisure accommodation In Ghana. Location only is minutes drive away from Ghana’s main airport, and boasts of free Wi-Fi internet access all through its premises.

All our rooms are designed with the business and leisure traveler in mind. Each room has an air-conditioner, flat screen TV with satellite channels and accessed with an electronic key lock, spacious and enclosed car park with 24 hour security at post.
